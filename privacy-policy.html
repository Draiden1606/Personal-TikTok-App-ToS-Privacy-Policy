Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Aiden's Content Assistant ("the App") handles information when connecting to TikTok's Content Posting API.

Who this app is for

This is a personal-use application built and operated by a single individual (Aiden Cague) to manage and publish content to their own TikTok account. It is not intended for use by other TikTok users, and it does not collect, process, or store data on behalf of any third party.

What information is accessed

The App connects to TikTok via OAuth 2.0 and, with explicit account authorization, may access:

  • Basic profile information needed to authenticate the connected account
  • The ability to upload and publish video content to the connected account

The App does not access, store, or share private messages, contact lists, or any data belonging to other TikTok users.

How information is stored

Authorization tokens are stored locally on the operator's own device using encrypted or environment-based credential storage. Tokens are never transmitted to any third party and are not stored on any shared or public server.

How information is used

Access is used solely to publish video content that the operator has personally created and approved for posting to their own TikTok account. No data collected through this integration is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Data retention

Video files and captions are retained locally only as long as needed to complete the publishing workflow. OAuth tokens are retained until manually revoked by the operator or until TikTok's token expiration policy takes effect.

Revoking access

The connected TikTok account holder may revoke this App's access at any time via TikTok's account settings under "Manage Third-Party Apps," or by contacting the operator directly.
